About

Christina graduated from The University of Alabama in May of 2019 with degrees in journalism and psychology. A week later, she drove 2,300 miles from her hometown of Louisville, Kentucky, to Seattle, Washington. Here, she works as an editorial assistant for a digital newspaper founded in 1863, the Seattle P-I. The paper is now a branch of Hearst Communications, one of the world's largest diversified communications companies.

Christina is now enrolled in the University of Washington's full-stack web development bootcamp, and also has extensive experience writing professionally for various travel snd lifestyle publications in cities like London and Seattle. Christina is particularly interested in digital content, web development, travel writing, and food writing. Learn more about her via her LinkedIn page, here.
